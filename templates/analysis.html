Technical Details

Model Architecture
  • Algorithm: Logistic Regression
  • Vectorizer: TF-IDF with 5000 features
  • N-grams: 1-2 words
  • Stop Words: English stop words removed
Performance Metrics
  • Training Accuracy: ~90%
  • Testing Accuracy: ~85%
  • Precision: ~86%
  • Recall: ~84%
How It Works

The model analyzes the text of job descriptions and identifies patterns that distinguish real job postings from fake ones. It looks at word choice, sentence structure, and linguistic patterns that are characteristic of legitimate HR communications versus scam attempts.
